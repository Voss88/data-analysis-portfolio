About Me

Experienced Data Analyst with a MSc in Data Science and Business Analytics. Currently, I am at the forefront of the UK automotive technology industry, where I utilize data science models, including Bayesian analysis, to evaluate A/B tests on AutoTrader's Search & Product Pages. My primary focus is on analyzing innovative website components created by the product team and assessing how these features enhance consumer journeys.

This comprehensive skill set allows me to effectively interpret complex datasets and provide actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

My previous experience in the social media and advertising technology sector involved optimizing marketing campaigns and improving brand performance through data-driven strategies. I excel at transforming complex data into clear, actionable strategies that drive business success.
